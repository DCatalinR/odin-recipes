Pasta al Pomodoro

Pasta al Pomodoro is a culinary masterpiece that encapsulates the heart of Italian gastronomy. This dish, known for its simplicity and rich flavours, showcases the artistry of perfectly cooked pasta embraced in a vibrant and aromatic sauce. The essence of Pasta al Pomodoro lies in its ability to deliver a harmonious blend of taste and texture, inviting you into a world where each bite carries the tradition and warmth of Italian cooking.

Ingredients per 1 person

  • 100 grams of Spaghetti
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish(optional)
  1. Blanch the tomatoes in boiling water for about 1 minute, then transfer them to an ice bath. Peel off the skin and chop the tomatoes.
    2. >
  2. n a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until it becomes fragrant but not browned.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan. Season with salt and black pepper. Cook for about 15-20 minutes, crushing the tomatoes with a spoon as they soften.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  5. Transfer the cooked pasta to the pan with the tomato sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly, allowing it to absorb the flavors.
  6. Add torn basil leaves to the pasta and sauce. Stir gently to incorporate the basil.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water gradually until you reach the desired consistency.
  8. Plate the Pasta al Pomodoro, garnish with additional basil leaves, and, if desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese.

Dive into the delightful simplicity of Pasta al Pomodoro. Buon appetito!
